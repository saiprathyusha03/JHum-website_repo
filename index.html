Music

Lyrics are the poetic expressions woven into music, giving voice to emotions, stories, and thoughts.
They have the power to resonate deeply with listeners, transforming melodies into meaningful experiences.
Whether simple or complex, lyrics connect with our hearts by capturing moments of love, pain, joy, or reflection, making songs memorable and personal.

I like simple songs and pretty words because they have a way of touching the heart without being complicated. There's beauty in simplicity, and it allows me to connect with the message easily. Simple songs often carry deep emotions, and pretty words can paint a picture that feels warm and comforting. Together, they create something special that lingers long after the song has ended.
